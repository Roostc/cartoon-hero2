Dobby the House Elf

About Dobby

Dobby is a house elf who was freed by Harry Potter from the service of the Malfoy family.

Dobby was freed when Harry tricked Lucius Malfoy into gifting the elf a sock

Dobby's Characteristics

  1. He has big ears
  2. His eyes are huge and round
  3. He collects and wears items of clothing gifted by his newfound friends

Dobby's friends

After Harry aided Dobby's escape from the Malfoy household, Dobby befriended numerous wizard, amongst whom - apart from Harry himself:

  • Ron Weasley
  • Hermione Granger
  • Albus Dumbledore
